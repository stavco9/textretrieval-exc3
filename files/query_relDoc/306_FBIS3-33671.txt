Article Type:BFN 



  [Text] The representatives of the [Algerian] Islamic 

Salvation Front [FIS] in exile have issued a communique claiming 

that, between 9 and 25 January, the Islamists [in Algeria] have 

killed 117 people, both civilians and soldiers. The FIS 

communique added that the movement was not claiming 

responsibility for these killings, but that it was simply 

publishing the figures to inform the public.
